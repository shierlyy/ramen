ABOUT US.

Established in 1910, ERamen was the pioner of ramen restaurants in Japan. ERamen specializes in serving authentic Japanese ramen with organic flour and other dishes. ERamen is one oldest ramen restaurant in the world. ERamen brought to Indonesia since 2015.

OUR VISION

To be the most desirable ramen restaurant in the world and to build on our achievement and exquisiteness in the Ramen Industry.

OUR MISSION

To consistenly provide our customer with impeccable service by our warmth and delicious work of ramen. To have every customer who comes through our doors leave impressed by ERamen specialities and will look forward to being with us again.
